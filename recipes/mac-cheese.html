Macaroni Cheese

Looking for a homemade alternative to boxed mac and cheese that's just as quick and easy? This stovetop mac and cheese uses staple ingredients and comes together in just 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in salted boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling
  3. Add cheese and stir until melted
  4. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce
